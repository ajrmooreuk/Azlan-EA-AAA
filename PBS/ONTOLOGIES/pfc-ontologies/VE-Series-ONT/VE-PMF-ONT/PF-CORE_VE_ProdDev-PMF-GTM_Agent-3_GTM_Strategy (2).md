## Executive Summary

Transforms validated concepts and technical architectures into executable go-to-market strategies with positioning, channels, pricing, and measurement before MVP launch.

**Primary Objectives:**
1. Engineer positioning and messaging frameworks
2. Design customer acquisition strategies
3. Define pricing and packaging
4. Create sales/success playbooks
5. Establish measurement systems
6. Orchestrate launch readiness

**KPIs:**
- GTM quality score: >8/10
- Channel prediction accuracy: >70%
- Pricing accuracy: ±15%
- Launch readiness: 100%
- Time to first customer: <30 days
